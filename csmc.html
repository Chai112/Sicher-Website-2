Construction Safety Management Consulting

Sicher supports our clients by overseeing the daily activities that are performed around construction projects.

Overview

There are many hazards for workers and those nearby in the construction sites. With over 30 years of knowledge and experience, Sicher supports our clients to oversee the daily activities around construction projects. With more demands from social and government occurring on job sites, our EHS experts support safety project planning, daily safety meetings, conduct audits of programs and activities and incorporate with construction managers and subcontractors.

Setup EHS Management System

We work with construction professionals to establish policies for better health and safety practices. We educate managers and encourage them to set the standard for their employees.

Job Safety Analysis (JSA)

Before starting tasks, the workers shall break the task down into steps, identify the associated hazards, and the corrective actions to either eliminate or protect themselves from the identified risks. Eventually, our construction safety consultants will evaluate and train individuals and workers who need guidance to improve the JSA process.

Operating Procedures

We develop EHS operating procedures for your construction projects. We want your employees to recognize and apply proper techniques and guidelines for completing a job. The operating systems are covered all Hot-work and Non-Routine Work Permits, Log-out Tag-out, Covid-19 prevention, and many more.

Observation Reports

Construction Safety Observation Reports are used as an informal way to conduct job site observations to identify the negative and positive acts or conditions related to construction site safety. Completing these forms is typically the responsibility of all front-line leadership and employees involved with the project.
